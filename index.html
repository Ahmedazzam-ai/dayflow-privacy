Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

DayFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address solely to personalize your experience within the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your name on the dashboard and profile screen
  • To identify your account and sync your tasks
  • We do NOT sell, share, or rent your data to any third party

Data Storage

Your task data is stored locally on your device. Account information is handled securely via Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication
  • Firebase Authentication — for secure login management

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by signing out of the app. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, go to Profile → Delete Account inside the app. This will remove your account from our systems and clear all locally stored information immediately.

Children's Privacy

DayFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
